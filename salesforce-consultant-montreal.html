What I Do

Hands-on Salesforce and Certinia work, from first implementation to ongoing optimization

Salesforce Implementation

Stand up Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, and Experience Cloud the right way — clean data model, sensible automation, and a configuration your team can actually maintain after go-live. I work end to end: discovery, build, data migration, testing, and user training.

Certinia PSA Implementation

Certinia (formerly FinancialForce) Professional Services Automation is my specialty. Resource management, project accounting, timecards, and revenue recognition — configured to match how your services business actually runs, natively on Salesforce.

Billing Automation

Turn manual, spreadsheet-driven invoicing into automated billing inside Salesforce and Certinia — milestone and time-and-materials billing, billing schedules, and revenue recognition that ties back to delivery. Fewer errors, faster cash, and a clean audit trail.

Agentforce & AI

Practical AI, not hype. I design and deploy Agentforce agents grounded in your Salesforce data — for case deflection, internal Q&A, and guided service — with clear guardrails so you know exactly what the agent can and cannot do.

Flow & Apex Development

When clicks aren't enough, I write code. Declarative Flow automation where it fits, and well-tested Apex (triggers, batch, integrations) where it's needed — always with proper test coverage and an eye on governor limits and long-term maintainability.

Integrations

Connect Salesforce and Certinia to the rest of your stack — accounting, payments, ERP, and custom systems — using REST/SOAP APIs, middleware, and MuleSoft, so data flows reliably instead of being re-keyed by hand.

Bilingual Delivery, Built for Québec

Working in Québec means working in French and English — often on the same project. I run workshops, write documentation, and train users in either language, and I configure Salesforce so your team uses it in the language they prefer. Bilingual page layouts, translated picklists, and French user training are part of the plan, not an afterthought.

As an independent consultant, you work directly with me — the person doing the build. No layers, no handoffs to an offshore team you never meet. That keeps communication tight, decisions fast, and accountability clear.
